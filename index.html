Frequently asked questions

Is this invoice generator really free?

Yes. Every feature — line items, tax, discounts, currencies, the live preview, and PDF download — is free with no sign-up and no watermark. There is no paid tier.

Where is my invoice data stored?

Only in your browser. The invoice PDF is generated locally on your device and drafts are saved to your browser's local storage. Nothing is uploaded to a server, and we never see your clients or amounts.

How do I create an invoice PDF?

Fill in your details, your client's details, and at least one line item, then click Download PDF. The whole process takes under a minute, and the live preview shows exactly what the PDF will look like.

Can I add tax or a discount to my invoice?

Yes. Set a percentage tax rate and an optional percentage discount in the invoice details. The discount is applied to the subtotal first, then tax is calculated on the discounted amount.

Which currencies are supported?

USD, EUR, GBP, CNY, JPY, AUD, CAD, and INR. The currency symbol and number formatting are applied consistently across the preview and the downloaded PDF.

Can I edit or reuse a previous invoice?

Your latest invoice is saved automatically in your browser on the same device. Reopen the site, bump the invoice number, adjust the line items, and download the new PDF — ideal for repeat clients.

Do I need to install anything or create an account?

No. It works in any modern browser on desktop or mobile with nothing to install and no account required.

Can I send the PDF invoice directly to my clients?

Yes. The download is a standard A4 PDF that opens in any PDF reader, so you can attach it to an email, share it via a messaging app, or print it.
