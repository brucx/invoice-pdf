Note: this is not a Vietnamese e-invoice (hóa đơn điện tử)

For domestic tax purposes, Vietnamese businesses must issue electronic invoices through providers connected to the tax authority (per Decree 123 and Circular 78). What this tool creates is a commercial invoice (hóa đơn thương mại) — the standard document for international trade, quotations, and payment requests.

Frequently asked questions

Do Vietnamese diacritics render correctly in the PDF?

Yes — ơ, ư, đ, ạ, ế and every other Vietnamese character render exactly, because the PDF embeds a Noto Sans subset rather than relying on whatever fonts the reader has installed.

Can I make a Vietnamese–Chinese bilingual invoice?

Yes. Choose Tiếng Việt as the primary language and 中文 as the second — labels print like "Đơn giá / 单价", and your content can mix scripts in the same line.
