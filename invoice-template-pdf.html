What's included in this invoice template

The template covers everything a standard invoice needs to get paid without back-and-forth:

  • Invoice number and dates — a unique invoice ID, issue date, and due date so payments are easy to track.
  • Your details and your client's — business names, addresses, and contact information in clearly separated From / Bill To blocks.
  • Itemized charges — description, quantity, and unit price for each line, with the amount calculated automatically.
  • Tax and discount — percentage-based tax rate and optional discount applied to the subtotal.
  • Total due — a bold, unmissable final amount, formatted for your currency (USD, EUR, GBP, CNY, JPY, AUD, CAD, or INR).
  • Notes — payment terms, bank details, or a thank-you message.

Why a PDF invoice template beats Word or Excel

A PDF looks identical on every device and can't be accidentally edited after you send it — which is exactly what you want for a billing document. Word and Excel templates break their layout the moment a description wraps to a second line, and they require software your client may not have. With this template you fill in a form, watch the live preview, and download an A4 PDF that opens anywhere, prints cleanly, and is ready to attach to an email.

Frequently asked questions

Is the invoice template really free?

Yes — free with no sign-up, no watermark, and no paid tier. Generate as many invoice PDFs as you need.

Can I edit the PDF after downloading it?

PDFs are intentionally hard to edit — that's a feature for billing documents. To make changes, reopen the generator (your draft is saved in your browser), edit, and download a fresh PDF.

Do I need Adobe Acrobat or any other software?

No. The template is filled out in your browser, and the downloaded PDF opens in any PDF reader, including the one built into every modern browser.
